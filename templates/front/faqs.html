Basic

Health Checks is a platform that monitors your con jobs and notifies you of their status.
This are tasks that are created on your server to run automatically after a period of time
It will send notifications through your preferred channel.
The notifications will be sent through email,sms, slack and telegram plus other common channels.
Yes it will nag you if you do not attend to a cron job that is down.
It will give you peace of mind as you are sure any down time will be reported immediately.

Mobile

When an email is registered any device connected to the health checks app is automatically syncronised.
The Site is modified to work on any App hence can be accessed on any mobile phone device..

Account

Payments are through mastercard, Visa, PayPal and American Express.
You can use it with any language that can fetch a url. Like python and ruby.
You can configure the period between each ping according to your preference.
